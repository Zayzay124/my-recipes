Salmon

Description

In this recipe we are going to make a simple salmon dish.

Ingredients

  • Salmon cut
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cumino
  • Lemon
  • Vegetable oil
  • Butter (tbs)

Steps

  1. Season salmon with seasonings listed above and let sit for at least 10 min
  2. Heat up pan to medium heat
    3. o
  3. Pour vegetable oil and butter on pan and move pan around till they are mixed together
  4. Place salmon on pan and let cook for 5 min with the scales down
