Trout

Description

This recipe works for any variation of trout(rainbow trout,lake trout, etc.)

Ingredients

  • Trout Fillet
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon
  • Vegetable oil
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Season trout with seasonings listed above and let sit for at least 10 min
  2. Heat up pan to medium heat
  3. Pour vegetable oil and butter on pan and move pan around till they are mixed together
  4. Place trout on pan and let cook until you see edges start to whiten
