Catfish

Description

This recipe covers how to properly fry a catfish.

Ingredients

  • Catfish fillet
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cumino
  • Lemon
  • Vegetable oil
  • Butter (tbs)

Steps

  1. Season salmon with seasonings listed above and let sit or at least 10 min
  2. Heat up pan to medium heat
  3. Pour vegetable oil and butter on pan and move pan around till they are mixed together
  4. Place salmon on pan and let cook for 5 min with the scales down
